Now implement:

Milestone 33A — Persistent Player Identity & Career Reputation

Purpose:
Create long-term player identity and operational reputation systems so the player feels like they are building a cybersecurity career inside a persistent operations environment.

Important:
Do NOT turn this into an RPG.
Do NOT add fantasy mechanics.
Do NOT add complex stat systems.
Do NOT add backend, AI, authentication, multiplayer, or economy systems.
Do NOT clutter the interface.

Core Design Goal:
The player should feel:

“My operational decisions define my cybersecurity reputation.”

NOT:

“I completed disconnected missions.”

---

1. ADD PERSISTENT ANALYST PROFILE

---

Create a compact persistent Analyst Profile section.

Display:

ANALYST PROFILE

Name:
Blue Team Analyst

Current Role:
Cybersecurity Intern

Operational Reputation:
Generating dynamically

Containment Rating:
Developing / Stable / Strong

Threat Response:
Developing / Improving / Reliable

Manager Trust:
Low / Moderate / High

Do NOT make this large.
Keep compact and professional.

---

2. ADD OPERATIONAL REPUTATION SYSTEM

---

Create lightweight reputation tracking.

Add reusable functions:

updateOperationalReputation()
calculateAnalystBehavior()
renderAnalystProfile()

Purpose:
The game should remember how the player behaves operationally across assignments.

---

3. TRACK PLAYER DECISION PATTERNS

---

Track behavioral tendencies such as:

* careful evidence handling
* fast escalation
* delayed escalation
* strong containment
* excessive containment
* accurate reasoning
* weak prioritization
* operational focus
* investigation discipline

Do NOT expose raw numbers.

Instead:
translate behavior into professional reputation language.

---

4. GENERATE REPUTATION TRAITS

---

Based on behavior patterns, assign lightweight reputation labels.

Examples:

* Reliable Investigator
* Careful Analyst
* Fast Responder
* Threat Stabilizer
* Evidence Focused
* Operationally Disciplined
* Developing Analyst
* Improving Responder

Do NOT:

* assign too many labels
* make them feel gamey
* create fantasy classes

Only show:
1–2 dominant operational traits.

---

5. ADD MANAGER TRUST EVOLUTION

---

Manager reactions should now reflect cumulative operational behavior.

Examples:

Positive:
“You consistently prioritize containment effectively.”

Concerned:
“We need stronger evidence discipline before escalation.”

Neutral:
“Your operational judgment is improving.”

This should evolve over time using:

* mission outcomes
* containment choices
* evidence handling
* analyst confidence
* decision quality

Do NOT use AI.
Use scripted behavior logic.

---

6. ADD PERSISTENT OPERATIONAL HISTORY

---

Add compact section:

Previous Operations

Examples:
✔ Credential Phishing Investigation
✔ Network Exposure Review
⚠ Delayed Containment Incident
✔ Threat Stabilized Successfully

Purpose:
Player should feel:
they have a growing operational history.

Keep compact and scrollable if needed.

---

7. ADD CAREER PROMOTION PROGRESSION

---

Current Role:
Cybersecurity Intern

Next Role:
Junior SOC Analyst

Promotion readiness should depend on:

* completed assignments
* trust score
* operational reputation
* analyst confidence trends
* containment effectiveness

Do NOT unlock promotion yet.
Only show progression toward it.

Promotion Readiness: 68%

8. ADD PERFORMANCE SUMMARIES AFTER MISSIONS

---

Mission completion summaries should now include professional interpretation.

Examples:

Operational Assessment:

* Strong containment timing
* Reliable evidence prioritization
* Good escalation discipline

or:

Operational Assessment:

* Escalation timing needs refinement

This should feel like:
professional evaluation.

---

The world should recognize player history.

Examples:

Operations Center:
“Previous phishing incident successfully contained.”

Manager:
“You handled the previous exposure review well.”

Threat Status:
“Blue Team readiness improving.”

This creates:
persistent world continuity.

## GOAL

The player should now feel like:

a persistent Blue Team analyst building a cybersecurity career and operational reputation inside an evolving cyber defense organization.

professional,

and progression-driven

creating clutter,
