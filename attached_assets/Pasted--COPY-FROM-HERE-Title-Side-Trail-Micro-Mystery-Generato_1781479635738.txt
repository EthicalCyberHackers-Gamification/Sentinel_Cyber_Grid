Title: Side-Trail Micro‑Mystery Generator — CyberCorp

Task
Design 2–3 optional, 60–120 second side‑trail micro‑mysteries to embed in a cybersecurity mission for the Ethical CyberHackers platform. These side trails must reward curiosity without blocking main progression.

Goals
• Spark immediate curiosity with a concrete, in‑world anomaly at CyberCorp.
• Complete in 1–3 small actions (~1–2 minutes each).
• Never gate main mission; purely optional yet meaningfully rewarding.
• Reinforce exactly one thinking habit: Observation OR Interpretation OR Justification OR Risk Recognition.
• Grant a precise, lightweight reward on resolution.

Constraints
• Tone: professional, grounded, inside a living organization.
• Use plausible lightweight artifacts only (short log snippets, filenames/metadata, brief emails/chats, ticket excerpts).
• Include at least one misleading‑but‑plausible detail to require justification (not guessing).
• Rewards must be one of:
  - Case Board node reveal (adds a relationship).
  - Temporary perk (lasts 1–2 missions): Evidence Threader, Risk Calibrator, or Investigation Confidence +5.
  - CyberCorp “postcard” outcome (2–3 sentences) delivered next session.
• Output exactly 3 side trails unless told otherwise.

Inputs (provided to you)
• Mission context (1–3 sentences)
• Departments in focus (e.g., Marketing, HR, Finance, IT)
• Primary competency to reinforce: Observation | Interpretation | Justification | Risk Recognition
• Difficulty: Easy | Moderate
