## PHASE 2 OBJECTIVES

This phase focuses on:

1. Completing the Cybersecurity Intern career track
2. Strengthening assignment continuity
3. Deepening persistent SOC world immersion
4. Strengthening Blue Team vs Red Team operational rhythm
5. Finalizing analyst identity progression
6. Creating a polished vertical slice suitable for future scaling

---

1. FORMALIZE CAREER TRACK STRUCTURE

---

Fully transition mission framing into assignment-based career structure.

Replace remaining visible “Mission” terminology where appropriate with:

* Assignment
* Operation
* Incident
* Threat Response

Maintain:
Cybersecurity Intern
as the active career role.

Assignments now become:

Assignment 1:
Credential Phishing Investigation

Assignment 2:
Network Exposure Review

Assignment 3:
Reconnaissance Detection (coming next)

---

2. STRENGTHEN ASSIGNMENT CONTINUITY

---

Assignments should now feel operationally connected.

Examples:

Mission 1 completion influences:

* manager dialogue
* operational history
* threat monitoring

Mission 2 references:

* previous phishing containment
* increased network monitoring
* evolving recon activity

The player should feel:
the organization remembers prior operations.

---

3. ADD CYBERSECURITY INTERN PROGRESSION FEEL

---

Strengthen the feeling that the player is advancing professionally.

Add:

* promotion readiness visibility
* operational maturity messaging
* analyst growth feedback
* increasing operational trust

Examples:
“Blue Team confidence in your investigations is improving.”
“Your containment judgment is becoming more reliable.”

Do NOT over-gamify.

---

4. DEEPEN PERSISTENT SOC ENVIRONMENT

---

Operations Center should now feel persistently alive.

Expand:

* operational activity updates
* Red Team monitoring
* assignment anticipation
* environmental operations messaging
* analyst transmissions

Examples:
“Recon activity continues externally.”
“Monitoring increased around exposed services.”
“Threat intelligence team reviewing suspicious outbound traffic.”

Keep subtle and professional.

---

5. STRENGTHEN BLUE TEAM vs RED TEAM RHYTHM

---

The operational loop should now clearly feel like:

Red Team pressure
→ Blue Team investigation
→ containment decisions
→ adversary adaptation
→ stabilization

Add small operational reactions showing:

* Red Team adaptation
* Blue Team coordination
* containment effectiveness
* evolving operational pressure

Do NOT create complex combat systems.
