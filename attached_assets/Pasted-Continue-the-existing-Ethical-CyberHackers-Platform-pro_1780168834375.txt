Now implement:

Milestone 28C — Cinematic Incident Interruptions

Purpose:
Make important cyber incident events feel emotionally impactful and attention-grabbing WITHOUT cluttering the interface or overwhelming the student.

Important:
Do NOT add giant popups.
Do NOT add constant flashing.
Do NOT redesign the entire app.
Do NOT add backend, AI, multiplayer, or sound.

CRITICAL:
Do NOT change:

* current system alert location
* current alert timing
* current alert queue behavior
* current toast positioning
* current alert durations

The current alert placement and timing system has already been tuned for readability and student visibility and must remain intact.

This milestone should ENHANCE the emotional feel of alerts using:

* subtle environmental reactions
* temporary cinematic emphasis
* background dimming
* mission pulses
* terminal pacing
* mission node reactions

NOT by relocating or retiming alerts.

Core Design Goal:
Important events should briefly take over player attention, then disappear cleanly.

The system should feel:
alive,
reactive,
and cinematic.

NOT:
spammy or cluttered.

---

1. USE EXISTING ALERT SYSTEM

---

Do NOT replace the current alert/toast system.

Instead:
layer cinematic effects AROUND the existing alerts.

Existing alerts should remain:

* in the same position
* same duration
* same animation timing
* same queue behavior

---

2. ADD CINEMATIC EMPHASIS LAYER

---

Create reusable function:

showIncidentInterruption(eventType)

Purpose:
Temporarily increase emotional weight around important events.

Behavior examples:

* slight background dim
* subtle terminal flicker
* mission node pulse
* threat indicator animation
* containment progress animation
* transmission pulse
* temporary UI focus emphasis

Do NOT create large new overlays.

---

3. INTERRUPTION SEVERITY TYPES

---

Severity types:

* info
* caution
* threat
* containment
* mission

Visual behavior only:
Do NOT modify alert location or duration.

info:
subtle cyan pulse

caution:
small amber pulse

threat:
brief red environmental pulse

containment:
green/blue stabilization glow

mission:
stronger cinematic mission transition effect

---

4. IMPORTANT EVENTS ONLY

---

Only apply cinematic emphasis to:

* major Red Team movement
* successful containment
* mission escalation
* additional employee targeting
* workstation isolation
* mission complete
* new assignment unlocked

Do NOT trigger cinematic emphasis for:

* every command
* every file open
* small XP changes
* small evidence updates

---

5. TERMINAL DRAMATIC PACING

---

During major interruption events:

* briefly slow terminal typing animation
* slightly dim terminal background
* then restore normal interaction

Do NOT freeze the app.
Do NOT interrupt gameplay for long.

Purpose:
Create dramatic pacing while preserving usability.

---

6. MISSION CONTROL REACTIONS

---

When major incident events occur:

* current mission node pulses briefly
* threat indicator animates
* containment progress visibly updates
* manager transmission indicator reacts shortly afterward

This should make the Mission Control environment feel alive.

---

7. MANAGER FOLLOW-UP

---

After major incident reactions:
manager should respond naturally.

Examples:

Threat escalation:
“We may be losing containment.”

Successful isolation:
“Good response. Threat spread appears reduced.”

Additional phishing:
“This may no longer be isolated to one employee.”

Keep responses:
short,
operational,
and emotionally grounded.

---

8. KEEP UI CLEAN

---

Rules:

* only ONE major cinematic emphasis event at a time
* no giant persistent overlays
* no alert spam
* no stacked cinematic interruptions
* no large dashboard growth

After each event:
the UI should calmly return to investigation mode.

---

9. LIGHT ENVIRONMENTAL MOTION

---

Add subtle environmental motion:

* mission node pulse
* transmission blink
* containment bar animation
* threat pulse
* terminal flicker during major events

Keep animations:
professional,
lightweight,
and readable.

---

MISSION COMPLETE
Threat Contained

Do NOT modify the existing alert timing/location behavior during this sequence.
