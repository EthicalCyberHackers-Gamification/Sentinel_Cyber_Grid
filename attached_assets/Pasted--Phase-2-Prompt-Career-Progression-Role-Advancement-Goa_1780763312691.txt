# Phase 2 Prompt — Career Progression & Role Advancement

Goal:
Transform mission progression from:
“unlocking levels”

into:

“advancing through a cybersecurity career.”

This phase adds:

* role progression
* promotion structure
* operational growth
* increasing responsibility

WITHOUT disrupting the current gameplay loop.

---

## WHAT STAYS THE SAME

Keep:

* current mission architecture
* terminal gameplay
* world map core structure
* assignment mechanics
* evidence systems
* scoring systems
* XP systems
* investigation systems

Do NOT:

* redesign gameplay flow
* rebuild missions
* replace terminal mechanics

This phase adds progression meaning, not new gameplay systems.

---

## WHY THIS FEATURE IS BEING ADDED

Current issue:

Mission progression currently feels like:
a sequence of assignments.

But the player should feel:
professional advancement.

Instead of:
“Mission 4 unlocked”

the player should feel:
“I earned promotion into a new cybersecurity role.”

This creates:

* long-term motivation
* identity progression
* operational realism
* emotional investment

---

## ROLE PROGRESSION STRUCTURE

Proposed role progression:

Tier 1:
Cybersecurity Intern

Tier 2:
Junior SOC Analyst

Tier 3:
SOC Analyst

Tier 4:
Incident Response Analyst

Tier 5:
Threat Hunter

Tier 6:
Cloud Security Analyst

Tier 7:
Senior Operations Analyst

---

## PROMOTION STRUCTURE

Promotions should happen after:
mission groups.

Example:
every 4 missions.

Promotion requirements may include:

* XP threshold
* successful containment
* evidence accuracy
* low false positives
* mission completion quality

---

## OPERATIONS CENTER ENHANCEMENTS

The world map / operations center becomes:
CyberCorp Operations Headquarters.

Add:

* current role display
* promotion progress
* assignment queue
* analyst division
* clearance level
* division status

Do NOT clutter the interface.

Keep everything operational and minimal.

---

## MISSION SCALING BY ROLE

Intern missions:

* guided
* narrow scope
* beginner analysis

Junior SOC missions:

* multiple hosts
* log analysis
* alerts
* correlation

SOC Analyst missions:

* broader campaigns
* multi-system investigations
* response decisions

Incident Response missions:

* active compromise
* containment
* escalation
* damage assessment

Threat Hunter missions:

* stealth persistence
* proactive detection
* hidden attacker behavior

---

## ROLE-SPECIFIC IMMERSION

Each promotion should subtly change:

* responsibilities
* assignment types
* mission expectations
* operational pressure

Do NOT dramatically change the UI.

Progression should feel:
professional.

NOT arcade-like.

---

## PROMOTION PRESENTATION

Avoid flashy cinematic promotions.

Instead use:
professional operational notifications.

Example:

CyberCorp Operations Notice

Based on your recent assignment performance,
you have been promoted to:

Junior SOC Analyst

New assignment categories unlocked:

* Multi-host investigations
* Alert correlation
* Incident escalation support

---

## SUCCESS CRITERIA

This phase succeeds if:

* progression feels like career advancement
* missions gain operational meaning
* players understand why difficulty increases
* immersion improves without complexity overload
* the game still feels clean and focused
* players become emotionally invested in advancement
