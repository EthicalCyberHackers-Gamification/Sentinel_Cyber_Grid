## MISSION BRIEFING ROOM PANEL

Add a new panel:

Mission Briefing Room

The panel should appear after mission selection but before the investigation starts.

Manager introduces the assignment.

---

## MISSION 1 BRIEFING ROOM

Manager Assignment:

"Finance has reported a suspicious file on an employee workstation. Before investigating, review the briefing materials and prepare your approach."

Show 3 Briefing Cards:

Card 1:
Phishing Indicators

Content:

* Requests for passwords
* Urgent language
* Unknown senders
* External email domains

Card 2:
Evidence Collection

Content:

* Gather facts first
* Do not assume
* Document findings
* Verify suspicious activity

Card 3:
Password Safety

Content:

* Passwords should never be shared
* External requests are suspicious
* Company policy should be reviewed

Student must review all 3 cards.

---

## MISSION 2 BRIEFING ROOM

Manager Assignment:

"A network alert has identified a potentially exposed host. Review the briefing materials before beginning your investigation."

Show 3 Briefing Cards:

Card 1:
IP Addresses

Content:

* Devices use IP addresses
* Analysts identify hosts before scanning

Card 2:
Network Reachability

Content:

* Reachability helps confirm targets
* Unreachable systems cannot be investigated directly

Card 3:
Open Services

Content:

* Services provide functionality
* Exposed services increase attack surface

Student must review all 3 cards.
